Music Influences Life

What is Music?

Music can mean alot of different things to people all around the world. Music is something respected in every nationality and every walk of life. Whether you are living in a penthouse in New York or on the streets of Africa music holds an important role. Often times poeple might not realize how often music is incorporated in their day to day life. When sitting down for a relxing show after a long day of work music holds an important role in the influence of your emotions. When the intro to your absolute favorite TV show starts playing you begin to relax and dive into the world created by the show directors. That intro music is specifically chosen to spark a certain emotion in the viewers that let's them know what they are beginning to watch. Let's take a deeper look into music and how it effects the lives of so many.
